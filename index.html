Brown Bear

Bears are extra-ordinarily intelligent and have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing.

  • Scientific Name: Ursus arctos
  • Average Weight: 240 KG
  • Average Lifespan: 25 Years
  • Habitat: Woodlands

A predominantly dark, richly brown colored bear possess far superior navigation skills compared to humans with excellent memories and large brain to body ratio. A fully grown brown bear can smell food, cubs, a mate or predators from miles away.
